This privacy policy applies to the My Pocket Guitar Tuner app
(hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created

---

## Microphone Access

The Application requires access to your device's microphone solely
to detect the pitch of guitar strings in real time. Audio is
processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored,
or transmitted to any server.

---

## Information We Do Not Collect

The Service Provider does not collect, store, or share any personal
information. The Application does not require account creation or
registration.

---

## Third Party Advertising

collect certain device information to serve relevant ads. Please
refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
