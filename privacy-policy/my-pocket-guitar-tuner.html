This privacy policy applies to the My Pocket Guitar Tuner app for mobile devices, created by Bugga Software Limited Company. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Microphone Access

The Application requires access to your device's microphone solely to detect the pitch of guitar strings in real time. Audio is processed locally on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to any server.

Information We Do Not Collect

Bugga Software does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The Application does not require account creation or registration of any kind.

Third Party Advertising

The Application displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children

The Application does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.
